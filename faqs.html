We offer both on-site and in-shop services. For many issues, we can come to your home or business to perform repairs and installations. Computer Repair

My computer is running very slowly. Can you fix this?

Yes, we can help with slow computer performance. This is a common issue that can have multiple causes, including:

  • Malware or virus infections
  • Too many startup programs
  • Fragmented hard drive
  • Outdated hardware
  • Insufficient RAM
Our technicians will diagnose the specific causes of your slow performance and recommend the most cost-effective solutions to speed up your system.

Can you recover data from a computer that won't boot?

In most cases, yes. Even if your computer won't start, the data on your hard drive is often still intact and recoverable. Our technicians use specialized tools and techniques to safely extract your important files, photos, and documents. The success rate depends on the specific nature of the failure, but we have a high recovery rate for most common scenarios. We always prioritize data preservation in our repair process.

How long does a typical computer repair take?

Most standard repairs can be completed within 24-48 hours. Simple issues like virus removal or software troubleshooting might be resolved the same day. More complex hardware repairs or data recovery could take 3-5 business days. We'll provide you with an estimated timeframe after diagnosing your specific issue, and we'll keep you updated on the progress throughout the repair process.

Virus & Malware Removal

How can I tell if my computer has a virus?

Common signs that your computer may be infected with a virus or malware include:

  • Unusually slow performance
  • Frequent crashes or error messages
  • Pop-up advertisements, even when not browsing the web
  • Programs starting or closing automatically
  • Changes to your browser homepage or search engine
  • Unfamiliar programs or toolbars appearing
  • Friends receiving strange emails from your account
If you notice any of these symptoms, it's best to have your computer checked by a professional as soon as possible.

What's the difference between a virus scan and professional virus removal?

A basic virus scan using consumer antivirus software can detect and remove many common threats, but it has limitations. Professional virus removal services like ours offer several advantages:

  • We use multiple professional-grade tools that can detect threats consumer software might miss
  • We can identify and clean infections in system areas that regular scans can't access
  • We repair damage caused by the malware, not just remove the infection
  • We implement preventative measures to protect against future infections
  • We can recover data that might be encrypted or hidden by ransomware
Our comprehensive approach ensures your system is truly clean and secure.
